Facilities

CODING

Extensive care is given to the students in terms of coding and lots of regular tasks and homeworks. One to one student to teacher tutorials are given when needed or if asked by the students. Surprise tests are taken or regular basis. Laboratory task are also given to the students and also teachers are active all over the labwork times.

LIBRARY

An extensive collection of books in our library for all sorts of subjects and topics for the students to indulge into. Every student can get into the library anytime they want and ask for a book to read or borrow to. The students can take any book they want and for any given amount of time.

PROJECTS

Projects are a very important part of our university. Projects with weekly deadlines are given to the students. The deadlines are very strict one. The projetcs are strictly aimed at the development of the students. The projects are given to the students by the faculty within our campus also from various respected alumnis.

TRAINING

The sole focus of the university if the developement of our students. For the better development of the students we encourage them to indulge into various trainings and courses to the students of the subjects they want to know or learn abou the subjects. Both teachers from within the university and outside the university are invited to teach the students and train them in their interested subjects.
